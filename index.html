About Me

My name is Sarah Peled. I am a Danish 22-year-old, currently studying Multimedia Design, at University College Lillebælt in Odense, Denmark. Studying Multimedia Design conists of going in depth with various fields, which means I have knowledgede and skills in different fields of work. I'm most passionate about web-development and web-design, which is why this field is my primary focus. Since I find it extremely annoying and frustrating when websites are confusing and difficult to use, I want to be able to help create websites that are user friendly. I like how this requires both being technical and being creative, as I have always been both.

I am definitely a logical and critical thinker, and I love learning new things. My favourite subjects in school were always math and science, and was never really fond of socialstudies or sports. Being creative is aanother quality of mine, and I enjoy being creative in many ways that not only include school and work. Since i can remember, I have always loved to draw and to paint, and this is where I find myself the most relaxed. I also enjoy baking, where I mostly love the decorating process, which is something I spend a lot of my free time doing.
